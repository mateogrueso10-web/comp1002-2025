About Classic Coca-Cola

History

Since its invention in 1886 by Dr. John S. Pemberton, Coca-Cola has become a symbol of refreshment around the world. Originally sold as a fountain drink, it quickly became a household name known for its unique flavor and invigorating taste.

Iconic Design

The classic glass bottle, introduced in 1915, became a design icon recognized worldwide. Its contoured shape ensures that Classic Coca-Cola stands out on any shelf and adds a touch of nostalgia to every sip.

Why People Love It

  • Timeless, crisp, and refreshing taste
  • Trusted and loved by generations
  • Perfect for sharing with friends and family
  • Symbol of celebration and enjoyment

Classic Coca-Cola is more than a drink—it’s a tradition and a cultural icon that continues to refresh millions every day.
