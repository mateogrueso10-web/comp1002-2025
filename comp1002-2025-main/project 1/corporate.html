The Coca-Cola Company

Company History

Founded in 1886 by Dr. John S. Pemberton in Atlanta, Georgia, The Coca-Cola Company has grown to become one of the most recognized and beloved brands worldwide. From its humble beginnings as a soda fountain beverage, Coca-Cola has expanded to include a wide range of products, all designed to refresh the world.

Our Mission

To refresh the world in mind, body, and spirit, inspire moments of optimism and happiness, create value and make a difference.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and fairness.
  • Quality: We are committed to delivering the best products.
  • Innovation: We continually seek new ways to delight our customers.
  • Community: We strive to give back and support the communities we serve.
